Hi,<br><br>
I'm a postgraduate student in Statistics and Mathematical Finance. I have been studying in London for four years. I received my [Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Economics and Finance from University College London](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/prospective-students/undergraduate/degrees/statistics-economics-finance-bsc), and my [Master's in Financial Mathematics in Cass Business School, City University of London](https://www.cass.city.ac.uk/study/masters/courses/financial-mathematics).

During my spare time, I usually travel around, do my workout in a gym, or simply stay at home and pick up some new knowledge and skills from [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/). I have great interests in stochastic analysis, modern probability, and their applications is financial mathematics. I'm now actively seeking a suitable PhD studenship in mathematics/ statistics (financial mathematics stream).

Research Interests
======
* Numerical methods approximating decoupled forward-backward stochastic differential equations (FBSDEs);
* Backward-stochastic differntial equations (BSDEs) driven by jump processes;
* Pricing and hedging in incomplete market;
* Machine learning in financial mathematics;
