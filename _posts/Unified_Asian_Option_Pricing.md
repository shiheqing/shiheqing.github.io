This is a derivation of unified arithmetic average asian option PDE, which is achieved by replicating strategy and change of measure. A proper change of measure leads the pricing problem to a simple one-dimensional PDE without drift, which can be done via numerical methods.

The original paper is written by Prof. Jan Vecer, Department of Statistics, Columbia University.

The full paper can be found here (http://shiheqing.github.io/files/asian-vecer.pdf)
