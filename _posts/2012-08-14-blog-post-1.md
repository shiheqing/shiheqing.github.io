This is an introduction about a novel regression based American options pricing method, which reduces the computaion costs of the traditional Monte Carlo methods. 

The original paper is written by Francis A. Longstaff and Eduardo S. Schwartz, Department of Insurance and Finance, UCLA.

[Full paper can be found here](http://shiheqing.github.io/files/LongstaffSchwartzAmericanOptionsLeastSquareMonteCarlo.pdf)
